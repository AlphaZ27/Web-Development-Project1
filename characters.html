Ellie Williams

Main Character

She is 19 years old in the second game.
She is Joel's psuedo-daughter
She and Dina are dating.
She is best friends with Dina and Jesse.

The protagonist of the game, and the main antagonist in Abby's perspective. Ellie is a 19-year-old survivor of the Cordyceps Brain Infection outbreak and the only person known to be immune to the infection. Ellie lives in a large community in Jackson. She sports a tattoo over the bite scar on her right arm to hide her immunity. After witnessing a horrific attack, Ellie embarks on a personal quest for vengeance.

Joel Miller

Character

He use to be a smuggler.
He is Ellie's psuedo-father.
He had a biological daughter, Sara but she died at the beginning of the first game.
His brother is Tommy.

Joel is Ellie's father figure and a former smuggler, who bonded with her years ago while transporting her across the country to the Fireflies in the hopes of creating a vaccine for the CBI outbreak. Unbeknownst to Ellie, Joel killed the Fireflies who were going to kill Ellie to create a cure.

Owen

Character

He is Abby's ex-boyfriend
He and Mel are dating.
He is the father of Mel's baby.
Was a member of the Fireflies.

Owen is a member of the WLF and Abby's childhood friend and ex-boyfriend. He is in a relationship with Mel and is having a child with her.

Manny

Character

He is friends with Abby.
He is a former member of the Fireflies.

Manny is a member of the WLF and a friend and roommate of Abby's. He accompanies her to Jackson and was later seen in the Seattle flashbacks.

Yara

Character

She is a former Seraphite.
She is the sister of Lev.

Yara is Lev's older sister who left the Seraphites with him, and is severely injured by them during their escape. She later accompanies Abby during the flashbacks of the latter's perspective.

Abby Anderson

Main Character

She is the daughter of the doctor from the first game.
She used to date Owen.
A former member of the Fireflies.
She was a member of WLF.

The false protagonist of the game. Abby is a member of the Washington Liberation Front, a former member of the Fireflies, and begins the game of attempting to locate a resident of the community in Jackson. Abby is forced to deal with the fallout of her own quest for revenge.

Tommy Miller

Character

His brother is Joel.
He is married to Maria.
One of the leaders at Jackson.
He is very good sniper.

Tommy is Joel's younger brother and is one of the leaders of the community in Jackson, alongside his wife Maria. Tommy is the only person aware of Joel's killing of the Fireflies and deception of Ellie. Tommy, like Ellie, spends the game on his own quest for vengeance.

Mel

Character

She and Owen are dating.
She is pregnant with Owen's baby.
She is a doctor.
Former member of the Fireflies.

Mel is a medic and a member of the WLF. She is pregnant with Owen's child, which causes tension between her and Abby.

Maria Miller

Character

She is one of the leaders at Jackson.
She is married to Tommy.

Maria is Tommy's wife, and a member of the Jackson community.

Dina is a close friend and love interest of Ellie and a resident of the community in Jackson. She is Jesse's ex-girlfriend, and kisses Ellie which causes a stir in the community. Dina accompanies Ellie throughout the game as their relationship develops.

Main Character

He is best friends with Ellie and Dina.
Father of baby JJ.
He use to date Dina.

Jesse is a friend of Ellie, and Dina's ex-boyfriend. He is a resident of the community in Jackson, and accompanies Ellie on her quest.

Nora

Character

She is a doctor.
She is friends with Abby
Former member of the Fireflies.

Nora is a member of the WLF and a friend of Abby's. She accompanies Abby to Jackson and is later sought out by Ellie.

Lev

Main Character

He is a former Seraphite.
He is the brother of Yara.
He is an archer.
He accompanies Abby.

Lev is a former member of a religious cult called the Seraphites set to wipe out all 'sin' in the world after the outbreak. Lev is branded an apostate by the Serephites for shaving his hair and is hunted by them alongside his older sister, Yara. Lev becomes Abby's companion and assists her.

Isaac

Character

Isaac is the leader of the Washington Liberation Front, and an antagonist in Abby's Seattle flashback scenes.
